# Jharkhand Tourism

## About
Welcome to Jharkhand Tourism! This website serves as a comprehensive guide to exploring the beautiful and diverse state of Jharkhand, located in eastern India. Our mission is to promote the rich culture, historical significance, and breathtaking landscapes that Jharkhand has to offer.

## Features
- **Attractions**: Discover popular tourist spots including waterfalls, national parks, and historical sites.
- **Accommodation**: Find a range of lodging options, from budget stays to luxury hotels.
- **Travel Tips**: Get essential information for travelers, including local customs, transportation, and safety tips.
- **Events and Festivals**: Stay informed about upcoming events and festivals that showcase Jharkhand's vibrant culture.
- **Photography**: Enjoy stunning visuals capturing the beauty of Jharkhand's landscapes and communities.

## Purpose
The purpose of Jharkhand Tourism is to raise awareness about the state's tourism potential and to provide visitors with all the information they need for a memorable journey. We believe in promoting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and preserves the natural beauty of Jharkhand.

Join us in exploring the wonders of Jharkhand and plan your next adventure!

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Authentication**: JSON Web Tokens (JWT)
- **Styling**: CSS, Bootstrap
